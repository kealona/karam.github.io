BFC规范理解

2022年01月21日 783 字 大概 4 分钟

什么是BFC

W3C对BFC的定义是：

In CSS, the adjoining margins of two or more boxes (which might or might not be siblings) can combine to form a single margin. Margins that combine this way are said to collapse, and the resulting combined margin is called a collapsed margin.

大意是：两个或多个普通流中的盒子在垂直方向上会外边距叠加

什么是普通流，除了float，root element，absolute和fixed定位的元素

什么时候会发生外边距重叠

分为两种情况：一种是父子元素的外边距重叠，一种是毗邻的元素
对于毗邻的元素，W3C的描述是：

Two margins are adjoining if and only if: - both belong to in-flow block-level boxes that participate in the same block formatting context - no line boxes, no clearance, no padding and no border separate them - both belong to vertically-adjacent box edges, i.e. form one of the following pairs:

top margin of a box and top margin of its first in-flow child
bottom margin of box and top margin of its next in-flow following sibling
bottom margin of a last in-flow child and bottom margin of its parent if the parent has “auto” computed height
top and bottom margins of a box that does not establish a new block formatting context and that has zero computed “min-height”, zero or “auto” computed “height”, and no in-flow children

  • 属于普通流的块级元素盒子且参与到相同块级格式上下文中
  • 没有被padding，border，clear，line box分隔开
  • 都属于垂直毗邻盒子边缘

如何避免外边距重叠

W3C中的描述为：

  • Margins between a floated box and any other box do not collapse (not even between a float and its in-flow children).
  • Margins of elements that establish new block formatting contexts (such as floats and elements with “overflow” other than “visible”) do not collapse with their in-flow children.
  • Margins of absolutely positioned boxes do not collapse (not even with their in-flow children).
  • Margins of inline-block boxes do not collapse (not even with their in-flow children).
  • The bottom margin of an in-flow block-level element always collapses with the top margin of its next in-flow block-level sibling, unless that sibling has clearance.
  • The top margin of an in-flow block element collapses with its first in-flow block-level child”s top margin if the element has no top border, no top padding, and the child has no clearance.
  • The bottom margin of an in-flow block box with a “height” of “auto” and a “min-height” of zero collapses with its last in-flow block-level child”s bottom margin if the box has no bottom padding and no bottom border and the child”s bottom margin does not collapse with a top margin that has clearance.
  • A box”s own margins collapse if the “min-height” property is zero, and it has neither top or bottom borders nor top or bottom padding, and it has a “height” of either 0 or “auto”, and it does not contain a line box, and all of its in-flow children”s margins (if any) collapse.

翻译下来是：

  1. float元素不会发生外边距重叠
  2. 创建BFC元素与子元素不会重叠
  3. absolute和fixed定位的元素不会重叠
  4. diaplay值为inlin-block的元素不会重叠
  5. 普通流中的元素永远与毗邻的元素发生外边距重叠，除非毗邻元素clear
  6. 普通流中的块级元素的margin-top和它的第一个普通流中的子元素发生margin-top重叠
  7. 普通流中的块级元素和它的最后一个普通流中的子元素发生margin-bottom叠加
  8. 如果一个元素的min-height为0，没有border，没有padding，高度为0或auto，不包含子元素，那它自身的外边距会发生叠加
